ABOUT KORAT REALITY

WHO
WE ARE.

Whether you're exploring investment opportunities, seeking secure NRI investments, or looking to maximize your land's potential, Korat Reality provides the expertise and structured processes needed for confident real estate decisions.

WHAT WE SPECIALIZE IN?

Land Acquisition & Due Diligence

Our land acquisition and development process begins with rigorous market research and location analysis. We evaluate infrastructure connectivity, proximity to economic hubs, future development plans, and demand-supply dynamics. Before any acquisition, our due diligence team conducts comprehensive legal verification including title searches, encumbrance checks, and ownership verification.

Land Development & Infrastructure

As a land development company, we transform raw land into development-ready properties. Our capabilities include layout planning, town planning approvals, infrastructure development, RERA compliance, and environmental clearances. We handle the complex regulatory and technical aspects so investors and landowners can focus on value realization.

Commercial, Residential & Industrial Projects

Our portfolio spans multiple segments: commercial developments (retail, offices, mixed-use), residential projects (gated communities, villas, apartments), and industrial developments (warehouses, logistics hubs, manufacturing facilities). Each project is evaluated based on market demand, location viability, regulatory compliance, and appreciation potential.

Investment Advisory & JV Models

Korat Reality offers property investment advisory India services including opportunity identification, risk assessment, portfolio strategies, and exit planning. For landowners, we structure joint venture real estate projects through JV and JDA models designed to maximize land value while ensuring fair revenue sharing and professional execution.
